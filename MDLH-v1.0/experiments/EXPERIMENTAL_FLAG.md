Status: `experimental`

Meaning:

- this repository contains a live research audit of multi-model theory growth,
- the physics material is not validated,
- the archive is intentionally used to test whether documentation can outrun
  independent confirmation,
- every new model contribution must be logged with source basis and status.

Operational rule:

- do not merge new theory claims into the main narrative without a dated
  lineage note.
