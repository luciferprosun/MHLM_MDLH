## Core Vision

Kimi’s vision is the most explicit mathematical roadmap for defending LSC in an experiment like BEST2. It treats the theory as salvageable, but only if it is upgraded into a proper EFT-style, flavor-aware, covariant, and statistically testable framework.

## How Kimi Develops LSC

Kimi pushes the theory in six directions:

- EFT with Wilson coefficients and a scale `Lambda`;
- explicit trace/anisotropy separation;
- flavor structure through the PMNS basis;
- full covariant tensor form;
- a proper likelihood and systematics model;
- direct predictions for BEST2, KATRIN, IceCube, KamLAND, and Daya Bay.

This is not just a comment layer. It is a roadmap for turning LSC into something that can survive a technical defense.

- systematics must be separated from the LSC template;
