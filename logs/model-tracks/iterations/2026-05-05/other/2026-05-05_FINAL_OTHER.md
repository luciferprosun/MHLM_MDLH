## Core Vision

This folder currently does not represent a distinct model line with its own theory contribution. It is a holding area for future models or manual review.

## How It Fits The Project

The role of `other` is archival discipline:

- keep future model outputs separate;
- prevent accidental mixing of unknown provenance into the main theory line;
- preserve a place for human review when a model does not fit Codex, GPT, Gemini, Manus, DeepSeek, or Kimi.

## MHLM Meaning

In MHLM terms, `other` is important because it keeps the archive honest about what is and is not assigned. It prevents false precision in the model lineage.
