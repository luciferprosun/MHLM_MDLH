# Model Response Evidence Policy

## Rule

Model responses in this repository are evidence of process, critique, prompt effects, disagreement, convergence, and contamination risk. They are not final authority.

## Model Responses Can Support

- reconstruction of research lineage,
- audit of prompt-response chains,
- comparison of critique patterns across models,
- identification of authority drift,
- documentation of hallucination and recursive consensus risks,
- freeze-readiness review.

## Model Responses Cannot Establish

- scientific validation,
- empirical truth,
- LSC physics correctness,
- AOIA runtime correctness,
- grant readiness by themselves,
- final methodology without human review.

## Promotion Rule

A model response may be cited in canonical methodology only as evidence of the review process. Any substantive claim taken from a model response must be restated in a human-authored methodology document with:

- source path,
- hash or stable archive reference,
- authority domain,
- explicit uncertainty,
- no consensus-as-validation language.

## Storage Rule

Final-round model reports are stored under `model-responses/`. They must not be silently merged into root doctrine, LSC theory files, AOIA runtime specifications, or active runtime code.
