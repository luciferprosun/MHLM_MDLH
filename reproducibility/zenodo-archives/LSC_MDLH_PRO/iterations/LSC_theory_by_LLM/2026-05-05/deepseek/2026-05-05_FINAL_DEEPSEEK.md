## Core Vision

DeepSeek is the red-team and formal-repair layer. Its view is that LSC is salvageable only if it is made mathematically clean, dimensionally consistent, and statistically identifiable.

It does not validate the theory. It identifies what must be repaired before LSC can be defended in a serious technical setting.

## How DeepSeek Develops LSC

DeepSeek’s main additions are structural:

- it exposes the tensor trace problem;
- it flags frame ambiguity for sidereal modulation;
- it points out the need for a proper likelihood and leave-one-out validation;
- it pushes the line toward EFT, flavor structure, and a covariant formulation.

DeepSeek is the model that most strongly turns LSC from a polished ansatz into a hard testable object.

## MHLM Meaning
