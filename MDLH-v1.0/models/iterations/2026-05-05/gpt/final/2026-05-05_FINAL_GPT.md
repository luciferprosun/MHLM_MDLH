# GPT Final Vision For MDLH

Date: 2026-05-05

GPT is the editorial and reframing layer.
It pushes the archive toward a detector-bias interpretation and improves the
readability of the project.

GPT mainly contributes by:

- moving away from PBH-centered framing;
- strengthening the detector-response story;
- cleaning up narrative structure;
- making the line easier to present in reports.

GPT makes LSC legible, but not validated.
