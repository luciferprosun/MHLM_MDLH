## Timeline

### 1. Early PBH / dark-neutrino phase

The earliest material is still ontologically heavy and astrophysical. It mixes dark-neutrino language, PBH modulation, SHDM origin language, and directional clustering ideas.

Evidence:

- `LSC_3_5_Model.pdf` describes a "Dark Neutrino Framework" with `theta_eff`, `Phi = -GM/r`, `Delta m^2 / (2E) = V_LSC`, and a SHDM + gravitational amplification conclusion.
- `pbhrm_3_3_publikacja.pdf` is a PBH regulatory model with a threshold response, delayed activation, environmental enhancement, and a tungsten-modulation hypothesis.
- `theory_overview.md` in `06_PBH_DARK_NEUTRINO_EARLY` states that LSC 4.2 extends neutrino oscillations into curved spacetime.

What this phase contributed:

- the first PBH-centered story,
- the first coupling between neutrino anomalies and environmental / geometric modulation,
- the first attempt to make the anomaly a structured framework rather than a one-off explanation.

What it did not yet have:

- a clean detector tensor,
- a separation between propagation and measurement,
- a stable public archive narrative.
