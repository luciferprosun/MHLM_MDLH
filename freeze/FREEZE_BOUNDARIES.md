# Freeze Boundaries

## Authority Separation

The freeze preserves these boundaries:

- LSC remains an external unvalidated case-study reference.
- AOIA remains an external runtime/provenance architecture reference.
- MHLM/MDLH remains an epistemic archive and methodology track.
- Legacy mixed roots remain historical lineage sources only.

## Forbidden Promotions

The following promotions are blocked:

- model response to scientific validation,
- LSC case study to MHLM canonical physics,
- AOIA runtime source to MHLM doctrine,
- mixed legacy root to active canonical source,
- recursive consensus to proof,
- archive existence to correctness.

## Handling Ambiguity

When authority is unclear, preserve the artifact, label it, and place it under human review. Do not silently merge it into canonical methodology.
